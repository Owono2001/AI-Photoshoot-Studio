How to Use This App

1. Get Your Image

  • Upload: Click 'Upload File' and select a PNG, JPG, or WEBP image.
  • Webcam: Click 'Start Webcam', allow permission, then click 'Capture Frame'. Click 'Stop Webcam' when done.

2. Edit Source Image (Optional)

Use the tools above the 'Source Editor' canvas:

  • **Select/Move:** Select drawings or text, then drag to move.
  • **Draw:** Draw on the image. Use controls for color/width.
  • **Text:** Add text. Double-click text on canvas to edit. Use color picker.
  • **Delete:** Select an item (), then click to remove.
  • **Clear Edits:** Removes only drawings/text, keeps base image.

3. Photobooth Fun! (Optional)

  • Ensure webcam () is active.
  • Choose Layout, Shots, GIF option first.
  • Click 'Start Photobooth'.
  • Follow countdown, pose for shots! Result appears on the right.

4. Apply Effects

  • Select an effect from the dropdown.
  • Adjust parameters if they appear.
  • Click 'Apply Backend Effect'. Be patient for AI effects.

5. Download Your Creation

  • Result appears in the 'Processed Result' box.
  • Click 'Download Result' below it.

Experiment and enjoy creating!
